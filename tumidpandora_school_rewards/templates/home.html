Task-a-Thon© Fundraiser

Simple Fundraising solution for schools

The lack of funds is one of the biggest stresses for schools! Task-a-Thon© empowers parents to support classroom development by creating tasks and paying $ rewards upon completion, enabling schools to fundraise all year round.

FIND MY SCHOOL LOG IN
  1. Manage all the tasks and earnings for your school in one place using the Tasks Dashboard. 
    2. Create a new task, add a reward, success criteria and assign it to one or more grades. 
    3. This screen shows details on a task including reward claims and discussions. 
  4. Parents can approve the reward once the task is marked as complete.
   5. Parents can approve or request more details once the task is marked as complete. 
   6. Once the reward is approved, it's time to pay the reward to your school. 
  School Admins can activate / deactivate or remove both parents and teachers from your school.
